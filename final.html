Discover the University of Liverpool

We are the Liverpool University. Located at the heart of a thriling world city,

we are a dynamic institution of academic excellence and innovation, making

positive impact accross the United Kingdom and the wider world

Since 1881, we've been at the forefront of world class research and education, known for our

cutting edge facilities and relentless pursuits of excellence and achievement. Find out how

you can be part of our vibrant community and explore the work we're doing with our famous

home city.

Our rankings and reputation

World Top 150

QS World Universiry rankings 2026

places us in the top 150 global

universities. We've moved up to

147th place in the world, climbing 43

from 165th in the 2025 rankings.

Our teaching is rated Gold, the highest level, by the

Teaching Excellence Framework.

We've climbed 5 places to 18th in The Times and The

Sunday Times Good University Guide 2026.

We’re ranked in the top 150 universities globally in the

Times Higher Education World University rankings.
